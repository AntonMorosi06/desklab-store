# DeskLab Store — Day Zero Action Plan

## Goal

The goal of Day Zero is not to make the store perfect. The goal is to get real human feedback and at least one serious product conversation.

## Exact actions

### 1. Open the live site

```text
https://antonmorosi06.github.io/desklab-store/
```

Check:

- homepage loads
- catalogue loads
- product request email opens
- mobile view is acceptable
- no obvious broken section

### 2. Choose the hero product

Use:

```text
Stand laptop pieghevole
Product ID: laptop-stand-01
```

Reason:

- easy to understand
- useful for students/workers
- coherent with DeskLab brand
- more valuable than tiny low-ticket products
- good first product for testing willingness to pay

### 3. Send the soft launch to 10 people

Use the message in:

```text
docs/soft_launch_messages.md
```

Audience mix:

- 3 friends/classmates
- 2 people who study/work at a desk
- 2 tech/productivity people
- 2 people who would give honest criticism
- 1 person who might actually buy

### 4. Record every response

Use:

```text
data/soft_launch_contacts.csv
```

If someone asks for a product, also record it in:

```text
data/orders_tracker.csv
```

### 5. Do not ask for payment too early

Before any payment:

- verify supplier
- calculate real margin
- verify shipping
- confirm conditions clearly
- consider legal/fiscal position before repeated sales

## Day Zero success criteria

Day Zero is successful if you get:

```text
10 people contacted
5 actual replies
3 useful feedback notes
1 serious product interest
0 fake promises
```

## What to do if nobody responds

Do not panic. Improve:

- hero copy
- product image
- price clarity
- trust section
- message quality
- audience targeting

## What to do if someone wants to buy

Do not improvise. Follow:

```text
docs/manual_order_workflow.md
docs/customer_reply_templates.md
docs/supplier_validation_checklist.md
data/orders_tracker.csv
```
