## Why this product first

The laptop stand should be validated first because it is the most coherent product for DeskLab:

- solves a visible desk problem
- useful for students, developers and remote workers
- easy to explain
- has a higher perceived value than small accessories
- fits the productivity/desk setup positioning
- can be shown visually in content

## Validation questions

Ask people:

1. Do you understand what the product is?
2. Would it improve your desk setup?
3. What price feels reasonable?
4. Would you request availability through this site?
5. What would stop you from buying?
6. Do you prefer a cheaper basic stand or a better-looking premium one?

## Supplier validation

Before selling:

- verify supplier cost
- verify shipping cost
- verify material
- verify stability
- verify weight limit
- verify dimensions
- verify return/refund risk
- verify delivery time
- verify image rights

## Sell / pause decision

Use this decision model:

```text
If people understand it + price feels acceptable + supplier margin works → keep as hero product.
If people like it but price feels high → test cheaper version.
If supplier quality is unclear → pause product.
If nobody cares → change hero product.
```

## Notes

Do not create payment links until the supplier has been checked.
