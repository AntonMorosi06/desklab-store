# DeskLab Store — First 7 Days Sales Plan

## Day 1 — Operational setup

- Verify that the GitHub Pages site is online.
- Verify all product request buttons.
- Verify that requests go to `desklabstore@gmail.com`.
- Prepare the order tracker.
- Prepare customer reply templates.
- Choose the first product to push: recommended product is the laptop stand.

## Day 2 — Product validation

- Review each product in `products.json`.
- Fill `data/product_validation_matrix.csv`.
- Remove or pause weak products.
- Pick 1 hero product and 2 secondary products.

## Day 3 — Soft launch

Share the site with a small trusted audience:

- friends
- classmates
- study groups
- people interested in desk setup
- productivity communities

Goal:

```text
20 visits
3 conversations
1 serious request
```

## Day 4 — Content test

Create simple content:

- one screenshot of the desk problem
- one short text about why the product helps
- one post showing the product page
- one message asking for feedback

Do not push aggressive selling. Ask for feedback first.

## Day 5 — First request handling

If requests arrive:

- enter them in `orders_tracker.csv`
- reply using templates
- validate supplier before payment
- do not rush payment

If no requests arrive:

- improve product description
- improve trust copy
- ask people why they did not request

## Day 6 — Improve offer

Based on feedback:

- adjust product names
- adjust price expectations
- improve FAQ
- clarify shipping and availability
- remove confusing language

## Day 7 — Decision

Evaluate:

- visits
- clicks
- email requests
- serious leads
- product interest
- supplier viability

Decision options:

```text
continue with same catalogue
focus only on one product
change product category
pause sales and improve trust
prepare v0.4.1 with request form
```
