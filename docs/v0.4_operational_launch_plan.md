# DeskLab Store — v0.4 Operational Launch Plan

## Objective

The v0.4 stage turns DeskLab Store from a documented static storefront into a controlled manual-order MVP. The goal is not to add automatic checkout immediately. The goal is to generate real product requests, validate supplier assumptions, track leads and decide whether a product is worth selling.

## Operational principle

DeskLab Store must stay honest and controlled:

1. Product prices are indicative until confirmed.
2. Availability is checked manually before payment.
3. Shipping time is confirmed manually before payment.
4. Payment links are not sent until product, supplier and conditions are checked.
5. Products with weak margin, unclear supplier conditions or high return risk are removed or paused.
6. No customer data should be collected beyond what is necessary for request handling.

## Current commercial model

The current model is a manual validation model:

```text
Visitor → Product interest → Email request → Manual supplier check → Final confirmation → Payment link only if safe → Manual order tracking
```

This model is slower than automatic checkout, but safer for an early project.

## First operating target

DeskLab should not measure success only by revenue at this stage. The first useful metrics are:

```text
100 visits
10 product clicks or strong product interactions
3 real email requests
1 safe validated sale
```

A sale is considered safe only when:

- supplier availability is confirmed
- estimated delivery time is confirmed
- customer receives clear conditions
- margin is acceptable
- payment is requested only after manual confirmation
- legal/fiscal situation has been reviewed before repeated commercial activity

## What to do before sending any payment link

Before sending a payment link, complete this checklist:

- identify the exact product
- verify supplier availability
- verify total supplier cost
- verify shipping cost
- verify expected delivery time
- verify return/refund risk
- verify product photos and descriptions are not misleading
- calculate real margin
- communicate final price and conditions clearly
- record the lead in `data/orders_tracker.csv`
- mark the product status in `data/product_validation_matrix.csv`

## What not to do yet

Do not add:

- automatic checkout
- fake stock counter
- payment buttons on product cards
- aggressive scarcity claims
- unrealistic shipping promises
- product claims you cannot verify
- analytics or tracking tools without privacy review
- customer database without privacy review

## v0.4 completion criteria

v0.4 is complete when:

- `data/orders_tracker.csv` is used for real requests
- `data/product_validation_matrix.csv` has reviewed products
- customer email templates are ready
- supplier validation checklist is ready
- site explains manual order flow clearly
- at least 3 real requests have been attempted or recorded
- issue #4 can be closed with evidence
