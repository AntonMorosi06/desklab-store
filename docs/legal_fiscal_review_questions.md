# DeskLab Store — Legal and Fiscal Review Questions

This file is a checklist for a professional review. It is not legal or fiscal advice.

## Business setup

- Can occasional test sales be handled without opening a structured business immediately?
- At what point does the activity become habitual/organized?
- Is a VAT number required before repeated online sales?
- Is registration with the Chamber of Commerce required?
- Is a SUAP/SCIA communication required for online retail activity?
- Which tax regime would apply if the project becomes continuous?

## Consumer protection

- What mandatory information must be shown before purchase?
- How should the right of withdrawal be communicated?
- What exceptions may apply?
- How must the legal guarantee be described?
- What terms are needed for shipping, returns and refunds?

## Privacy and data

- What personal data is collected through email requests?
- Is a privacy policy required even without analytics?
- What changes if a form service is added?
- What changes if analytics, pixels or advertising tracking are added?
- What data retention period is appropriate?

## Payments

- Can payment links be sent manually after confirmation?
- What payment provider is safest for early testing?
- What invoice/receipt obligations apply?
- What should be stored for accounting?

## Supplier and fulfillment

- Who is responsible if the supplier delays?
- Who is responsible for defects?
- What happens if the supplier product is different from the listing?
- Should some products be excluded due to quality, safety or certification risk?
